Moving Sorsogon Forward

Getting around Sorsogon is about to get a whole lot easier! We’re working to make transportation faster, smarter, and more eco-friendly—so whether you’re commuting to work, running errands, or just exploring the city, you’ll have better, more reliable options to get where you need to go.



    Less Traffic, Safer Roads

    See real-time traffic updates all across Sorsogon City and know the best route before you take out.

    • Real-time traffic updates - traffic density insight.
    • Smart traffic lights - AI-powered signals that adjust based on road conditions.
    • Safer roads - Better pedestrian crossings (footbridge or pedestrian overpass), clearer signs, and accident reporting systems in secluded areas.



    Better Public Transportation Experience

    We're upgrading public transport so it’s more efficient, comfortable, and convenient, especially for commuters and also to minimize traffic.

    • Smart Bus Stops - official loading and unloading bus stops and scheduled stops for each route.
    • E-jeepneys and e-trikes - implementation of emission standards and alternative transportation for clean, quiet, and eco-friendly transportation.
    • Fare System - uniformed mode of payment, pay with a tap of a card.



    More Ways to Move, More Ways to Live

    We believe that transportation is not only transactional but also recreational.

    • Bike lanes & bike-sharing - Not just travel but also adventure and healthier movement.
    • EV charging stations - Supporting electric vehicles for a cleaner future.
    • Walkable streets - Safer, wider sidewalks with shade and resting areas.
